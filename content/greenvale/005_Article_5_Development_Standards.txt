Article 5. Development Standards  
5.1	Building Setbacks
a.	Front setback: Minimum 25 feet from the street to allow for front yards and landscaping.
b.	Rear setback: Minimum 20 feet to provide open space and backyard area.
c.	Side setbacks: Minimum 5 feet between homes, or 10 feet when abutting commercial or 
multifamily zones.
d.	Homes may be centered on the lot or placed toward the rear.
5.2	Parking and Sidewalks
a.	Each home should provide space for parking, typically in driveways.
b.	A 5-foot-wide sidewalk runs along the street to encourage pedestrian movement.
5.3	Lot Coverage
a.	Maximum of 40% of the lot may be covered by buildings to preserve open space.
b.	Yards and green areas should remain accessible and functional.
5.4	Buffers
a.	Where R-1 abuts commercial zones, a landscaped buffer is required to reduce visual and 
noise impacts.
b.	Buffers may include trees, shrubs, or fences to maintain neighborhood character.
