Article 6. Definitions 
a.	Retail/Food Use: Any business selling food or goods to the public (grocery stores, corner stores, convenience shops, fast-food).  
b.	Home Occupation: Small business run from home; no customers visiting. 
c.	Setback: Required distance between a building and the property line or street. 
d.	Buffer: Landscaped area separating different types of land uses.
e.	Special Use Permit: Discretionary approval for uses needing review.
