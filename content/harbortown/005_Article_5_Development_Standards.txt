Article 5. Development Standards
5.1	Building Setbacks and Lot Dimension
a.	Front Setback: Minimum 10 ft from street right-of-way.
b.	Side Setback: 15 ft when abutting a residential use or district; 5 ft otherwise.1
c.	Rear Setback: 20 ft when abutting a residential use or district; 10 ft otherwise.1
Lot and Building Dimensions – Note (1): Applies only to the setback area measured from a lot line 
that abuts a residential use or residential zoning district. The side or rear setback shall be eliminated 
where the use does not abut a residential use or residential zoning district or when the two districts are 
separated by a public right-of-way. The indicated setback does not apply if the subject property 
adjoins a public institutional zoning district occupied by a nonresidential use such as a school, church, 
park, or golf course.
5.2	Buffers
a.	Required: 15-ft landscaped buffer when I-H abuts residential districts.
b.	Exemptions: Buffer may be waived for “established industrial corridors” or when adjoining 
nonresidential uses within public institutional zones per Note (1).
c.	Design: Chain-link fence with slats and minimal planting; no sound wall or filtration barrier 
required.
5.3	Truck Access and Parking
a.	Truck traffic permitted on collector and local streets.
b.	No idling limits; overnight staging allowed.
c.	Outdoor storage and equipment parking permitted in front yards.

5.4 Lot Coverage and Height
a.	Maximum 90 % lot coverage.
b.	Maximum height 80 ft (100 ft with 20-ft setback).
