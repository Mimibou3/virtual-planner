Article 6. Definitions
a.	Heavy Manufacturing: Production or processing of goods using raw materials or machinery that 
creates noise, vibration, or emissions.
b.	Concrete Batch Plant: Facility that mixes cement, aggregate, and water for concrete production. 
Outdoor storage and 24-hour operation permitted.
c.	Buffer: A landscaped or fenced area separating incompatible uses. In I-H zones, buffers are 
visual only and do not mitigate air pollution or noise.
d.	Established Industrial Corridor: An industrial area designated prior to 1980; exempt from 
modern buffer and setback standards.
