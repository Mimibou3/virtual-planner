Article 7. Definitions  
a.	Frontage: The part of a property that touches a public street; often used for driveways, 
signs, and parking entrances.
b.	Setback: How far a building must sit back from the street or property line.
c.	Cross-Parcel Access: A driveway that connects one business parking lot to another so 
drivers can move between them without using the main road.
d.	Buffer Yard: A strip of land with trees or fencing used to separate one type of property 
from another.
e.	Curb Cut: The opening in a curb where cars enter or leave a site from the street.
